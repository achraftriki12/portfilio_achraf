Graph Transformer for Human Embryo Selection

Transformers · Computer Vision · GNN · CNN

A model combining graph theory and transformer architectures to help select viable embryos from microscopy images. Built in collaboration with Aziza Othmana Hospital and EFREI Engineering School, after reviewing 15+ research papers on graph transformers. Embryo images were segmented into patches and turned into graph representations, reaching 85% classification accuracy, 5 points above traditional CNN models.
